Projects


hackBCA

I am currently one of the directors at hackBCA, a premier high school hackathon that occurs once a year at my school. My responsibilities include the livestream, coordinating with the school tech department for internet and power, as well as getting sponsors. This year, there will be 400 High School students in attendance who will spend 24 hours creating a game, app, hardware project, or any technology oriented project.

Water Conservation System

The water conservation system is an undersink device used to detect and stop the flow of water. This not only conserves water but can stop a sink from overflowing, causing damage to the home if left running accidentally. The WCS was developed in the Mechatronics Research Lab at The Bergen County Academies and won two awards at the North Jersey Regional Science Fair as well as being a winner of the North Jersey Steam Tank Competition. Also, check it out on git hub.

Lady Savant Foundation Website

A website I created and curently host and maintain for the Lady Savant Foundation, a foundation with a new mission each year. This year's mission is to build a school in Cambodia.

The 8-Bit Processor Project

The 8 Bit-Processor Project is a physical version of an 8 bit processor created on breadboards. Mine was created using matericals from a professor at Redeemer College, my computer science teacher, and Ben Eater's 8-Bit processor. Eventually, this project will be extended into designing a subset of the 6502 processor, which will be used as a curriculum for an elective at my school.
