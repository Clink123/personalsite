About Me

I am a student currently attending Bergen County Academies and I am really passionate about Computer Science. I have learned a few languages and dabbled in some others, as well as attending quite a few hackathons and making some awesome projects along the way. Check out my projects below!

Projects


hackBCA

I am currently one of the directors of hackBCA, a premier high school hackathon that occurs once a year at my school. This year, there will be 400 High School aged students in attendance who will spend 24 hours creating something awesome.

Positivity

Positivity is a website built for uplifting or positive thoughts. Users go on the site and can post uplifting thoughts which are placed on the main page. Users can then like the most uplifting thoughts and they will rise to the top of the page.

Lady Savant Foundation Website

A website I built for the Lady Savant Foundation, a foundation with a new mission each year. This year's mission is to build a school in Cambodia.

Water Conservation System

The water conservation system is an undersink device used to detect and stop the flow of water. This not only conserves water but can stop a sink from overflowing, causing damage to the home if left running accidentally. The WCS was developed in the Mechatronics Research Lab at The Bergen County Academies and will be attending the 2017 North Jersey Regional Science Fair. Also, check out the instructables.

BCA Apps

I am currently a working on an assortment of internal signup and request applications for my school. You can check it out on GitHub.

Nerdy Valentine Amazon Echo App

An Amazon Alexa app that gives the user a nerdy pickup line when asked. It is based on the Space Facts example program and was my first Amazon Alexa App. This won best application at Code Day 2016 and is currently published on the Alexa Skills store.
